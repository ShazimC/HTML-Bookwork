Chapter 3: Lists

Ordered Lists

The ordered list is created with the ol element.

Each item in the list is placed between li tags.

  1. Chop potatoes into quarters.
  2. Simmer in salted water for 20-30 minutes until tender.
  3. Heat milk, butter and nutmeg.

Unordered lists

The unordered list is created with the ul element.

  • First let me hop.
  • Out the muhheckin porsche.
  • Iowanna hit them textbooks.
  • So that I can become smarter respectively.

Definition Lists

The definition list is created with the dl (definition list) element and usually consists of a series of terms and their definitions. Inside the dl elements you will usually see pairs of dt and dd elements.

dt: definition term, is used to contain the term being defined.

dd: definition definition is used to contain the definition.

Sashimi
Sliced raw fish that is served with condiments such as shreded daikon radish or ginger root, wasabi and soy sauce.
Scale
A device used to accurately measure the weight of ingredients.
A technique by which the scales are removed from the skin of a fish
Scamorzo
An Italian cheese usually made from whole cow's milk (although it was traditionally made from buffalo milk).

Building an Arduino LED Dice

Here we will tell you how to make an Arduino programmed LED Dice.

Equipment needed:

  • 1 Arduino Uno.
  • 7 LEDs.
  • Pack of Jumper Wires.
  • 1 Breadboard.
  • 4 1K ohm Resistors.
  • 1 10K ohm Resistor.
  • Arduino Button.
  • Circuit Schematic.
  • Arduino Code.

Procedure:

  1. Assemble the 7 LEDs with 3 above, 1 in the middle and 3 below.
  2. Plug in the 1K resistors into their positive terminals on the breadboard.
  3. Plug in the button on the breadboard and plug the 10K resistor into its input terminal.
  4. Now use the jumper wires to connect the button and LEDs to the arduino following the schematic provided.
  5. Connect the arduino to your computer.
  6. Download the code provided to the arduino.
  7. Press the reset button on the arduino.
  8. Press the button on the breadboard to shuffle the dice and recieve a number between 1 and 6.
  9. ENJOY!
